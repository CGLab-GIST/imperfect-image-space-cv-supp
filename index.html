Overview

In this interactive viewer, we compare global illumination (high-dimensional) results of our imperfect control variates with those of the baseline control variates method and input-dependent uncorrelated weighting (IDUW) [Back et al. 2023], which uses the same input as our method. For direct illumination (low-dimensional), we compare our approach with regression-based Monte Carlo [Salaün et al. 2022], which uses perfect control variates (e.g., polynomials).
